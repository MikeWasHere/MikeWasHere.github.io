About Us

Our goal is to address the health and fitness needs of a buildings demographics to create a residential fitness amenity that provides a more affordable and convenient alternative to big box and corporate gym experiences. Our services include one-on- one personal training, group classes, customized meal plans and more.With 15 plus years of experience in the health and fitness industry, our team is comprised of motivated individuals who all bring different skill sets and knowledge to the table. We take a client focused, results driven approach to ensure each client is satisfied with their experience from start to finish,regardless of their goal.

Anthony Cassagnol

Personal Trainer, Account Acquisition, Program Design.

Anthony Cassagnol is an international fitness trainer and life coach who has been changing people's  lives one pound at a time. He is from Miami, FL,. As a co-founder of FITHAUSINT. Anthony's mission is to inspire and motivate men and women to live a healthier lifestyle.  His life's motto which he also applies to his love of fitness training is "Repetition inspires perfection".

Anthony graduated from Coral Reef Senior High School. Where he was on the track and field, football team, but where he excelled was basketball. After attending a 2 years at University Central Florida earning credits in business and psychology, he was unsure of what to do but he was always interested in keeping his body in shape and basketball. This  love, attracted people to him with questions about basketball and fitness training.

This motivated Anthony  to earn his Master Personal Trainer certification from AAAI. He combines a number of different training methods such as Athletics Training, High Intensity Interval Training (HIIT), Muscle Building, and Weight Loss to help his clients achieve their goals. He believe that core training is the foundation  and the best way to get clients to their goals effectively and safely.

Anthony's  knowledge and creativity has led him down several pathways to success. He takes his vast knowledge of fitness and tools for living a healthy lifestyle out the gym, going food shopping and making house calls, to cook breakfast and speaks to people about fitness all across south Florida.

When he is not training he enjoys spending time with writing, traveling and getting involved with the community, which includes personally taking time to seat and talk to the elderly, kids and homeless.
